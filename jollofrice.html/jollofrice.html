Above is the picture of a cooked jollof rice

The jollof rice is a meal served in occasion because its a food enjoyed by so many africans

The food can be served with chicken,fish, beaf, egg and so on

INGREDIENT

  • rice
  • pepper
  • salt
  • maggi
  • locust beans
  • stalked fish
  • tyme & curry
  • vegetable oil

STEPS TO COOK THE FOOD

  1. pour little vegetable oil inside a pot on fire
  2. pour your blended pepper inside the pot
  3. put in your seasons (maggi,salt,locust beans,tyme& curry)
  4. pour your rice

    5. Allow it to get boiled and serve with a cold drink
